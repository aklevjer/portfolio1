About me

Hey, I'm Aleksander, a 33-year-old from Porsgrunn, Norway. Having spent over a decade working as a chef, in early 2023, I felt it was time to change things around.

With a lifelong passion for IT, in the Spring of 2023, I enrolled in Noroff's front-end development course, following my dream to turn my passion into a fulfilling career.

While my core knowledge lies in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I've also been delving into PHP, SQL, C#, C++, C, Lua, React, TailwindCSS, and SCSS on a hobbyist basis.
